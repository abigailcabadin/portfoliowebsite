Hello! I'm Abigail Cabadin, a passionate data analyst dedicated to turning raw data into actionable insights. With a strong background in SQL, Python, R, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, I thrive on uncovering patterns and trends that drive informed decision-making. My journey in data analytics has been fueled by a love for problem-solving and a commitment to continuous learning.

I am transitioning from being a high school science teacher to a data analyst. I started my transition in 2023 by taking the Data Analyst Bootcamp by Alex the Analyst. Recently, I completed the Google Data Analytics course. Since then, I’ve been documenting my learnings and journey through GitHub repositories and LinkedIn posts. My skills include SQL, Python, R, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI for extracting, analyzing, and visualizing data to deliver actionable insights.

In addition to my technical skills, I bring a wealth of soft skills from my teaching background, such as effective communication, problem-solving, and the ability to explain complex concepts in an understandable way. These skills have been invaluable in my data analysis work, allowing me to collaborate effectively with team members and present data-driven insights clearly.

I am passionate about continuous learning and sharing my knowledge with others. I enjoy exploring new data visualization techniques and staying updated with the latest trends in data science. Explore my portfolio to see how I transform data into impactful stories.
