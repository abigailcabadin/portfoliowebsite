Hi! I’m Abigail Cabadin Beavers — a data analyst who brings nine years of science teaching experience to every project.

My journey into data began in 2023 when I completed Alex The Analyst’s Data Analyst Bootcamp, followed by the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. I’ve since earned the Microsoft Power BI Analyst and IBM Data Analyst credentials, and I’ve built a portfolio of end-to-end analytics solutions on GitHub.

With expertise in SQL, Python, R, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, I turn complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. My background as an educator sharpened my ability to:

  • Communicate results in plain language
  • Guide stakeholders through data-driven decision-making
  • Break down technical concepts into teachable moments

I thrive on solving real-world problems, whether automating data pipelines or designing interactive dashboards that highlight trends and uncover opportunities.

Explore my portfolio to see how I blend analytical rigor with creative storytelling — and let’s talk about how I can help your team harness the full power of data.
